01

A Short, Extraordinary Life

1991
Born in London on May 3 to Italian parents; baptized weeks later, then raised in Milan.
age 7
Asks for early First Communion — an unusual request granted after the parish priest questions him and finds him ready. Begins daily Mass, a habit he never breaks.
age 12–14
Becomes a catechist to his peers; teaches himself computer programming and web design; builds websites for his parish and school.
2004–2006
Spends over two years building a website cataloging more than 150 Eucharistic miracles worldwide, in nearly 20 languages — publicly unveiled October 4, 2006, days before his death.
Oct 12, 2006
Dies of an aggressive leukemia at age 15, ten days after falling ill. Buried, at his own request, in Assisi.
2020
Beatified in Assisi after a Brazilian boy's healing is recognized as a miracle attributed to his intercession.
Sept 7, 2025
Canonized by Pope Leo XIV — the Church's first millennial saint.

The Prediction

// recorded by Carlo in Assisi, three months before his death
> playing recovered file...
"When I reach 70 kilos, I am destined to die."
// 70kg ≈ 154 lbs — his exact weight at death

Days after Carlo's funeral, his mother Antonia woke to a voice saying "Testament." Searching his room, she found nothing written — but on his computer, the instrument he used most, she discovered a short video he had filmed alone three months earlier. He looked calmly at the sky and named the exact weight he would be when he died. He was.
